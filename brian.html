About the Show

Invincible follows Mark Grayson, a teenager who discovers he has inherited his father’s superpowers. His father, Omni-Man, is the world’s most powerful hero, but Mark’s journey to becoming a hero is filled with challenges, twists, and shocking revelations. Can he handle the weight of his father’s legacy, and how will he deal with the dark side of heroism?

Why You Should Watch

With its bold animation style, complex characters, and a storyline that keeps you on the edge of your seat, Invincible is one of the most gripping animated series out there. Expect intense action, emotional depth, and unexpected twists. Perfect for fans of superhero stories with a mature and gritty edge.
